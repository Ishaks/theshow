Executive summary

  • The internet has democratized content access and programming so that consumers are in control
  • The shift in the content consumption from broadcast to one-to-one has resulted in the rise of personalized content recommendation systems such as Netflix and Spotify.
  • Consumers want to access a diversity of content, while distributors want to minimize costs by keeping diversity low.
  • Key to ensuring that content recommendation systems evolve democratically is to innovate on monetization models.

Number of appearances in Top 100

  • This chart shows the top artists compared with Spotify vs Billboard in 2017 according to Spotify data.
  • Chainsmokers and Ed Sheeran run the best places followed by other artists.
Number of chart appearances on Spotify vs Billboard

  • The visualisation displays the number of top 100 chart appearances on both, Spotify and Billboard.
  • The placements are similar though Billboard is a bit more progressive than Spotify.
Charts appearance of Ed Sheeran's songs

  • Here we see the course of positioning that the most famous Ed Sheeran songs took.
  • Particularly Shape of You seems to have been very famous.
Top Artists by playing time

  • The most played artists in 2017 are displayed here.
  • Ed Sheeran ran the most time which correlates with the charts.
Correlation between music track properties

  • Shown are the correlations between certain music track properties.
  • Energy and loudness are highly correlated. Loudness and speechiness are negatively correlated.
Total Number of Movies released per Year

  • Years 2016 and 2015 had comparatively more movies than other years.
  • That entails that contribution of the movies to recommender system can be expected slightly more from those years.
Movies per Maturity Rating (2017)

  • Movie content of Netflix is divided in 13 Maturity rating categories.
  • Majority of content offered by recommendation system is TV-14, followed by PG.
Average User-Rating for Movies (2017)

  • Netflix collates the data of all movies rated in 2017, providing a basis for understanding the User rating to the content distributed across the maturity rating within the system.
  • Optimum rated movies are TV-14 meaning the movies approved for everyone.
Word Cloud for Movie Titles (2017)
